Promotion Retreat

Promotion Retreat is a yearly christian programme hosted by Taberah Glory Camp for over 25 years now.
In Promotion Retreat God comes to his people in a spectacular way, and he sends his word through his servants to heal them, deliver them, promote them and most of all give them a bigger and better understanding of who they are in the spirit realm and the change they can make on earth.

Promotion Retreat 2022

In 2019 God came and he gave us one word "OBEDIENCE" that was the theme of that years retreat, the year after he told us "we are the light of the world" but he did not stop there, in that year's retreat he gave us an instruction to obey he said to us "Let your light shine..." so we used what we learnt in 2019 to practice the message of 2020. But God did not stop there, he went ahead in 2021 and said "REIGN ON EARTH". He said now that we are light it will be easy for us to reign, because we are in such a dark world and light will always rule over darkness. Now God has come to us in 2022 and he has said "The Manifold Wisdom of God" he has come again this year to tell us another way to reign over principalities and power. God has come to clearly say that his plan for our lives is that we will be teachers, who will teach principalities and powers, the manifold wisdom of God (Ephesians 3:10).
